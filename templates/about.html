Good Morning!

Make your mornings run smoothly with the morning organizer for New Yorkers.

About Good Morning!

Good Morning! was created by Dorit Rein, Victoria Greene, Jason Luo and Raymond Lam. It was designed to help frazzled, tired New Yorkers plan out and organize their mornings, and if they so desire, their entire days. With Good Morning! all the information you need to know before you leave the house is right in front of you, on one easy-to-read page. The site uses the Pure CSS library and APIS from Weather Underground and the MTA.

How to use Good Morning!

When you log in to Good Morning!, you will be taken to your homepage, which displays the date and time, the current local weather, MTA updates, and your upcoming events. Your homepage links to a calendar, which you can update with all of your events and reminders.
